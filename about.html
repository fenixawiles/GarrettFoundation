About Peggy Garrett

Dedicated healthcare professional with over 15 years of experience helping families navigate senior care decisions and connecting healthcare facilities with exceptional talent.

Professional Background

A career built on compassion, expertise, and results

Healthcare Industry Expertise

With over 15 years in the healthcare industry, I have developed deep expertise in senior care placement, healthcare recruitment, and diagnostic testing coordination. My passion lies in helping families make informed decisions during challenging times while ensuring healthcare facilities have access to qualified professionals.

Core Specializations

  • • Assisted Living Facility Assessment & Placement Services
  • • Alzheimer's and Cognitive Testing Coordination
  • • Healthcare Professional Recruitment for Senior Housing
  • • Family Consultation and Care Planning

My Approach

I believe that every family deserves personalized attention and expert guidance when making important healthcare decisions. My approach is built on understanding each unique situation, providing clear information, and supporting families through every step of the process.

Whether you're seeking the right assisted living facility for a loved one, need specialized testing services, or are a healthcare facility looking for qualified professionals, I'm here to provide the expertise and support you need to make confident decisions.
